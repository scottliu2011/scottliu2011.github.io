Premier jour en classe de français. | The first day in French class.
Bonjour. Comment allez-vous? | Good morning. How are you going?
Bien, merci. Et vous? | Good, thanks. And you?
Très bien. | Very good.
Salut. Ça va? | Hi. How are you?
Oui, et toi? | Nice, and you?
Je m'appelle Pascal. Et toi? | My name is Pascal. And you?
Moi, c'est Alice. | Me, My name is Alice.
Bonne journée, ciao! | Have a good day, bye!
Salut, à demain. | Bye, see you tomorrow.
